How To Resize A Column

Drag: grab the thin handle on a header's edge and drag to resize the column.

Keyboard:

  1. Tab to a column's header.
  2. Hold Alt and press Left Arrow / Right Arrow to resize the column in small steps.
  3. Press Alt + Home / End to jump to the minimum or maximum width.
