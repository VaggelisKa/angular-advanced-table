Column Resizing Feature

Demonstrates interactive column resizing with pointer drag and keyboard support. Resizing is opt-in per column via enableResizing — toggle the columns below to add or remove their resize handles. Resizing is pixel-exact in both width modes. Under Fill (the default), columns stretch to fill the container and resizing a column reflows the others so the table stays filled. Under Fixed, column widths are authoritative and the region scrolls horizontally.

How To Resize A Column

Drag: grab the thin handle on a header's edge and drag to resize the column.

Keyboard:

  1. Tab to a column's header.
  2. Hold Alt and press Left Arrow / Right Arrow to resize the column in small steps.
  3. Press Alt + Home / End to jump to the minimum or maximum width.
